Who We Are

MINT is a modern private wealth platform built on alignment and fair access.

MINT exists to redefine how ambitious individuals grow, manage and leverage capital. We combine disciplined investment strategies, transparent data and intelligent technology to provide structured access across asset classes without the traditional opacity of asset management.

The Performance Model

At our core, we believe wealth management should be performance-driven, not product-driven. That is why we operate on a performance-only fee model. We earn when you earn. No hidden incentives. No unnecessary complexity.

Through curated OpenStrategies, real-time tracking tools, portfolio-backed lending and seamless capital mobility, MINT delivers more than investment access. We provide financial architecture.

Our Leadership

The Architects

Lonwabo Damane, CA(SA)

Co-founder & CEO

Lonwabo is a Chartered Accountant and serial entrepreneur focused on building transparent financial infrastructure. He completed his articles at Absa, where he was named Trainee of the Year, and later joined the Markets business in the CIB division, covering banks as a rated equity analyst. Combining deep public markets expertise with hands-on venture building, he brings disciplined insight and founder-led execution to shaping and driving MINT’s strategy.

Eletu Silo, CA(SA)

CFO

Eletu Silo is a Chartered Accountant (CA(SA)) with a strong foundation in external audit, financial reporting, and corporate governance. He brings a wealth of professional experience gained through his tenure in the auditing profession, most recently serving as an Audit Manager at EY.

In this role, Eletu was responsible for managing multiple audit portfolios across a diverse range of industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and financial services. His experience has equipped him with a deep understanding of complex business environments, regulatory requirements, and the practical application of financial reporting standards. He has successfully led audit teams, engaged with senior stakeholders, and ensured the delivery of high-quality, compliant audit outcomes.
